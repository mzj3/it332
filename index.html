Hacking Technique #1


Phishing is a deceptive technique where attackers disguise themselves as trustworthy entities to trick individuals into divulging sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, or financial details.

Typically delivered through emails, messages, or fake websites, phishing relies on social engineering to exploit human psychology.
